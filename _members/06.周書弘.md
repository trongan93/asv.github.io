北科大太空所碩士生，指導老師 裴重恩 教授


**周書弘**目前就讀國立臺北科技大學太空系統工程研究所，大學畢業於機械與機電工程學系，具備影像伺服控制、機電整合及自走車(AMR)之實作開發經驗。目前研究主要方向為基於深度學習的感測器融合技術，專注於透過深度學習演算法提取雷達(Radar)與光學影像(Camera)特徵，進行融合後部屬於FPGA、Jetson Nano嵌入式平台，並應用於無人機(UAV)之物件偵測。該研究旨在克服單一感測器在惡劣環境下的物理限制，以提升系統在複雜環境的感知精確度。

**Shu-Hong Chou** is a *Master's student in the Graduate Institute of Space Systems Engineering at National Taipei University of Technology*. He graduated from the Department of Mechanical and Mechatronic Engineering and has practical development experience in visual servo control, mechatronics integration, and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMR). His current research focuses on deep learning-based sensor fusion technology, specifically extracting features from Radar and Camera images using deep learning algorithms. These fused features are then deployed on FPGA and Jetson Nano embedded platforms for object detection in UAVs. The research aims to overcome the physical limitations of single sensors in harsh environments to enhance the system's perception accuracy in complex environments.
