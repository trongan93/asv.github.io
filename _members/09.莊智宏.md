北科大太空所碩士生，指導老師 裴重恩 教授

**莊智宏**目前就讀於國立臺北科技大學太空系統工程學系碩士班，大學畢業於大同大學電機工程學系。他的研究興趣在於將電機工程的專業知識應用於太空系統中，特別關注 AIoT 與訊號處理技術的整合。

**Chih-Hung Chuang** is a *Master's student in the Department of Space Systems Engineering at National Taipei University of Technology*. He received his Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Tatung University. His research focuses on integrating electrical engineering principles into space system applications, with a particular interest in AIoT and signal processing.
