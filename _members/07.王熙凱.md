北科大太空所碩士生，指導老師 裴重恩 教授

**王熙凱**目前就讀於國立台北科技大學太空系統工程系碩士班，在裴重恩教授的指導下，專注於影像人工智慧技術的研究。他目前參與一項基於衛星影像的辨識計畫，並對設計輕量化網路、提升模型細節還原能力，以及確保其在實際應用中的穩健適應性抱持濃厚興趣。此外，他致力於將所開發的 AI 模型實現於 FPGA，以達成邊緣裝置所需的高效能與低功耗硬體加速。

**Hsi-Kai Wang** is a *Master's student in the Department of Space Systems Engineering at National Taipei University of Technology*, focusing on research in image-based artificial intelligence technologies under the guidance of Professor Chung-En Pei. He is currently participating in an image recognition project using satellite imagery and is particularly interested in designing lightweight networks, improving model detail restoration capabilities, and ensuring robust adaptability in real-world applications. Furthermore, he aims to implement the developed AI models on FPGAs to achieve high-performance, low-power hardware acceleration required for edge devices.
