北科大太空所碩士生，指導老師 裴重恩 教授


**黃翊安**目前就讀於國立台北科技大學太空系統工程研究所碩士班，專注於 AI 演算法與硬體實現的交叉領域研究。目前的研究主題圍繞在 AI 演算法在硬體平台上的實現，將 AI 模型（如影像處理、深度學習等）轉化為實際的硬體設計。主要涵蓋演算法優化以及硬體描述語言：前者針對硬體實現，進行模型輕量化、量化（Quantization）等設計；後者使用 Verilog HDL 進行 RTL (Register Transfer Level) 設計，並實作於 FPGA 平台。

**Yi-An Huang** is a *Master's student in the Graduate Institute of Space Systems Engineering at National Taipei University of Technology*, focusing on the intersection of AI algorithms and hardware implementation. His current research centers on implementing AI algorithms on hardware platforms, transforming AI models (such as image processing and deep learning) into practical hardware designs. His work covers algorithm optimization and hardware description languages. The former involves model lightweighting and quantization for hardware implementation, while the latter involves RTL (Register Transfer Level) design using Verilog HDL and implementation on FPGA platforms.
