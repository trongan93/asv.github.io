## 🛰 活動簡介 | About the Seminar

本次專題演講將探討地球觀測太空系統在資料量、品質與即時性需求提高後，衛星與星座任務在設計與操作上面臨的複雜度挑戰。  
This seminar will discuss how Earth observing space systems have become increasingly complex as requirements for dataset quantity, quality, and timeliness continue to grow.

Dr. Selva 將介紹其團隊在衛星與星座自主化設計與操作上的最新研究進展，包含強化學習、多目標最佳化、自主感測網路，以及分散式任務分配方法。  
Dr. Selva will present recent progress toward autonomous spacecraft design and operations, including reinforcement learning, multi-objective optimization, autonomous sensor webs, and decentralized task allocation.

## 👨‍🏫 講者簡介 | About the Speaker

**Dr. Daniel Selva** 是一位專注於太空系統、人工智慧與系統工程交叉領域的學者與研究者。他專長於自主、決策支援系統，以及太空系統設計與操作。

他於 **麻省理工學院（Massachusetts Institute of Technology, MIT）** 獲得太空系統博士學位，曾任教於 **康乃爾大學（Cornell University）**，並曾擔任 Ariane 5 航電專家，參與 21 次成功發射任務。他也是 **AIAA** Associate Fellow，並發表超過 100 篇同儕審查論文。

**Dr. Daniel Selva** is an academic and researcher working at the intersection of space systems, AI, and systems engineering. He is an Associate Professor of Aerospace Engineering at **Texas A&M University** and Director of the **SEAK Lab**.

He earned his Ph.D. in Space Systems from **MIT**, previously served as an Assistant Professor at **Cornell University**, and worked as an Ariane 5 avionics specialist contributing to 21 successful launches. He has authored more than 100 peer-reviewed publications and is an **AIAA Associate Fellow**.
