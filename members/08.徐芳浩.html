徐芳浩 FANG-HAO HSU Master Student 碩士生 National Taipei University of Technology

北科大太空所碩士生，指導老師 裴重恩 教授

徐芳浩是國立台北科技大學太空系統工程研究所碩士班學生，大學畢業於電機工程學系。目前進行的研究主題為基於 AI 的演算法部署，專注於將影像處理與壓縮還原等演算法，移植並實現在嵌入式 FPGA 系統上。

研究興趣包括用於高效數據處理的人工智慧驅動技術，以及將AI模型部署到邊緣裝置的完整流程，目標是在 FPGA 或嵌入式系統上實現高性能和實時應用。

Fang-Hao Hsu is a Master’s student in the Graduate Institute of Space Systems Engineering at National Taipei University of Technology. He graduated from the Department of Electrical Engineering. His current research focuses on AI-based algorithm deployment, specifically on transplanting and implementing algorithms such as image processing and compression/restoration onto embedded FPGA systems.

His research interests include AI-driven techniques for efficient data processing and the complete workflow of deploying AI models to edge devices. His goal is to achieve high-performance and real-time applications on FPGA or embedded systems.
